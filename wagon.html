Wagon Detection

Real-time AI solution for railway wagon tracking and monitoring

Overview

Adapt AI’s Wagon Detection solution utilizes cutting-edge artificial intelligence to provide real-time tracking and monitoring of railway wagons under diverse conditions. Leveraging advanced video analytics and object recognition, this system ensures accurate identification and classification, making it a vital tool for railway operations, freight management, and logistics optimization.

Key Features

High Precision Tracking

Tracks wagons accurately in various lighting and weather conditions using AI-driven video analysis.

Real-Time Processing

Processes live camera feeds or video files with rapid inference, enabling instant wagon monitoring.

Flexible Deployment

Supports on-premise and cloud deployment, with customizable alerts and integration into railway systems.

Automated Classification

Automatically classifies wagons (e.g., loaded, empty, engine) with high reliability.

Output Details

Our Wagon Detection system delivers detailed outputs to enhance railway operations, including:

  • Wagon Type: Classification (e.g., Loaded, Empty, Engine, Brake Van).
  • Timestamp: Exact time of detection for each wagon.
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates or track segment ID.
  • Confidence Score: Percentage indicating the system’s confidence in the detection.

Use Cases

  • Railway Operations: Optimize train scheduling and wagon management with real-time data.
  • Freight Monitoring: Ensure accurate tracking of cargo across rail networks.
  • Maintenance Scheduling: Identify wagon conditions for timely maintenance and safety checks.
